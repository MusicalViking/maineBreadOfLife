How to Volunteer

Soup Kitchen

Volunteers are needed to serve in our soup kitchen! We serve breakfast and lunch restaurant style and need YOU to help make a difference.

Volunteer shifts: 8:00-10:00 AM for breakfast and 10:00 AM-1:30 PM for lunch. Or join us for the whole day!

Special Projects

Occasionally the Bread of Life needs larger projects tackled by caring individuals and businesses. These are perfect opportunities for groups, teams, or companies looking to make a significant impact.

Projects can include:
  • Landscaping
  • Painting shelter rooms
  • Painting apartments
  • Flooring installation
  • Cleaning
  • Gardening
  • Organizing donations
  • And much more!

Bread of Life relies on it's community for Volunteers and Donations to be able to help those in need, join today!
